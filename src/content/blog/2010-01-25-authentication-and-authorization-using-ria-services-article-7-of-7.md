<p>This article is very short.&#160; In the actual presentation, there was not much time to talk about this so a brief overview was done.&#160; Basically, itâ€™s all standard WCF stuff.&#160; The idea is that the Silverlight Business Template adds logging in and supports Authentication just like an asp.net application does.&#160; It uses the DomainDataSource to do the bridging between the silverlight clientside app, and the web application.&#160; Authorization is built into the login module also so that you can assign attributes to your domainservice classes to restrict access.</p>
