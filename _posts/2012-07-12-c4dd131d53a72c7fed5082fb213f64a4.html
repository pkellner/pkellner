Good afternoon! I recently found your resume online and would like to learn more about your background and your qualifications appear to be a good match.

Ext.js UI Developers – 2 positions – 1 year- Team of 5

Position Description

Serve as a Senior JavaScript UI Developer for a Global Investment Banking firm located in New York, NY to join a team working to extend an existing social networking portal for 18,000+ financial advisors. The Developer will work in a highly collaborative, cross functional and Agile team and may take a lead role on various software components. The Developer will also be responsible for developing unit testing and overall integration tests for the project team.

Essential Duties/Responsibilities:

· Designs and codes application components in an Agile environment utilizing a test driven development approach

· Assists in the creation and maintenance of project tasks and schedules

· Assists in providing programming estimates identifying potential problems and recommending alternative solutions

· Works in close cooperation with Delivery Leaders and other functional team members to form a team effort in development

· Collaborates with other members of the product development team

· Uses pair programming approach to ensure consistently high quality code

· Performs analysis and debugging

· Participates in and may lead weekly iteration demos for product managers

· Reports status of assigned software development and/or maintenance tasks

· Consistently follows software development methodology

Skills/Knowledge/Experience:

Required

· Advanced Javascript skills / Web UI Development Experience

o Experience using 3rd-party object-oriented JavaScript and its’ various flavors (AJAX, jQuery, extJS, etc.)

o Use Javascript to create interactive interfaces

o Create custom JavaScript Libraries

o Write Javascript utilities

o Use Javascript to make AJAX calls

o Use JavaScript with Cross Browser Compatibility

o Experience with HTML5, CSS3

· Bachelor degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent combination of education and recent, relevant work experience

· Minimum 7 years software development experience including 4 years of web development / Javascript / UI programming experience

· Minimum 1 year of hands on experience working in an Agile development environment that utilizes test driven development, continuous integration and pair programming

· Strong familiarity with design patterns such as MVC architecture model

· Thorough understanding of and experience in object oriented design and programming

· Experience using source code control systems, such as Git, Subversion, or CVS

· Knowledge of XML parsing, and XSLT

· Excellent verbal and written communication skills required

· Creative problem solving skills and ability to effectively communicate and translate feedback, needs and solutions

· Must have strong teamwork orientation and the ability to foster collaboration within and across teams

· Needs good work ethic and strong sense of ownership of end result

Preferred

· Understanding of Web Standards, Accessibility, and command of Semantic markup (XHTML, HTML5)

· Experience with high performance web page optimization techniques (edge serving, minimize http connections, etc.)

· Experience with a variety of Database Systems, such as LDAP, Oracle, Sybase, MongoDB, NoSQL

· Leadership skills and experience managing the work of development staff

To discuss this opportunity further, please contact me at your earliest convenience. Please send me a word copy of your resume to review.

Regards,

Michelle
