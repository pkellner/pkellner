April 19, 2007 11:30AM to 1:30PM US Pacific Standard Time

Mark Your Calendars!

Ask All Your Questions Real Time!

On April 19th at 11:30 AM PST, I will be joining a panel of ASP.NET experts  in a live chat to answer as many questions as we can for two hours.  For  those of you that are MVP's and don't know about this, it is a great  opportunity for us to interact with our peer MVP's.  For ASP.NET users at large,  this is a great opportunity to get your questions answered.

The format is  basically as you'd expect a chat room to be.  You post your questions and  the panel of experts answers them.  The reason this is so successful is  that the Microsoft Chat Software allows the experts (us MVP's) to grab questions and answer them.  Our first chat did not have this software working well and was
kind of chaotic.  Now that the software is working, it allows for very smooth flow of questions and answers.  In addition, the experts are all on a conference phone line so that when we get stumped, we can all talk about the
issue to insure the best answer is delivered.

This will be my third time participating in these chats and believe me, it's worth the time.  Come join the fun!  I wish when I was learning ASP.NET this was available.  It would have been a huge help.

The URL you should go to is this: 

http://msdn2.microsoft.com/en-us/chats/default.aspx.  From there, you should be able to find the link to the ASP.NET MVP Chat.  Hope to see you there.
